## What is missing from the current portfolio

The project has been heavily focused on SV-axis co-pole statistics. Missing directions with high potential:
1. **Spatial topology of the SV geometry** — the co-pole test collapses a 4D structure to a binary; what is the actual geometry? Is it a simplex, a cone, clusters?
2. **Layer transition dynamics** — SV4 drops after L6, SV3 has kinase-to-immune transition. What is the layer-wise trajectory of each gene in SV-space?
3. **Negative-space characterization** — what do genes in SV2/3/4 "neither" group encode? Is it cell-surface, secreted, metabolic?
4. **Within-complex coherence** — STRING has complexes; do all members of a known complex collapse to a single co-pole point?
5. **Cross-model alignment** — the geometry found in scGPT should be testable in Geneformer; this would validate that the signal is in the biology, not the model architecture.
